AI & BIG DATA FOR A SMARTER FUTURE EXHIBITION 2023

The world is becoming more hyper-connect with data every day. Data literacy is so critical to our education system but also key to humanity. 2023.01.19(THUR) ~ 20(FRI)

@ Riverside Drive, Nairobi, Kenya.
